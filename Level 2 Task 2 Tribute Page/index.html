Sidharth Shukla

Sidharth Shukla (12 December 1980 – 2 September 2021) was an Indian actor, host and model who appeared in Hindi television and films. He was known for his roles in Balika Vadhu, Broken But Beautiful 3 and Dil Se Dil Tak.[1] He emerged as the winner of reality shows Bigg Boss 13[2] and Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi 7. He hosted Savdhaan India[3] and India's Got Talent.
